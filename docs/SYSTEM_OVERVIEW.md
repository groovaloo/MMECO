# Moral Money – System Overview

Moral Money is a decentralized community coordination system designed to enable economic cooperation without surveillance, bureaucracy, or constant assemblies.

The system combines four core components:

1. **AI Agents**  
   Autonomous agents validate work, evaluate contributions, monitor the economy, and coordinate governance events.

2. **Blockchain (Substrate)**  
   Provides an immutable ledger where reputation updates, BuildCoin issuance, project validation, and governance decisions are permanently recorded.

3. **Reputation System**  
   Reputation is domain-based (construction, agriculture, energy, governance, health, logistics) and reflects demonstrated expertise rather than social status.

4. **Territory-Backed Economy (BuildCoin)**  
   BuildCoin is an asset-backed community currency linked to productive work, infrastructure, and territorial development.

## Governance Model

Governance is minimal and event-driven.

Most community activity happens without voting.

When disputes occur:

1. The system identifies the relevant domain.
2. The top five experts by reputation are automatically selected.
3. Experts evaluate the situation independently.
4. The decision is recorded on the blockchain.
5. The council dissolves immediately.

## Privacy Philosophy

The system deliberately avoids surveillance.

There are:

- no cameras
- no behavioral monitoring
- no constant tracking

Verification relies on voluntary proof submission, AI validation, and immutable blockchain records.

## Goal

The objective of Moral Money is to create a system where individuals remain free while economic cooperation, fairness, and accountability emerge from transparent infrastructure rather than centralized authority.
